Deep dive · LeadFlow Pro

From manual prospecting
to autonomous lead generation.

A full breakdown of one system — to show how I think, architect, and ship.

The Setup

Sales teams were burning hours on manual prospecting — with nothing reliable to show for it.

Enterprise BD teams were spending 60–70% of their week researching companies, verifying contacts, and assembling lead lists — most outdated by the time they reached the rep. No scalable system existed to discover and qualify leads continuously and consistently.

~70%Prospecting time eliminated
Real-timeContinuous discovery
5+ sourcesEnriched per lead
Zero-touchCRM-ready output
The Problem
No structured pipeline existed to find, qualify, and enrich company leads at scale. Each rep was using Google, LinkedIn, and intuition — leading to inconsistent data, wasted time, and missed pipeline.
The Solution
An autonomous AI agent pipeline that discovers target companies via web search, enriches them with contact and firmographic data from multiple sources, then delivers structured, sales-ready records into Sheets or CRM — zero manual intervention.
